2 INTRODUCED BY G. HERTZ

4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT PROVIDING FUNDING FOR REIMBURSEMENTS FOR FELONY
5 CRIMINAL JURISDICTION ON THE FLATHEAD INDIAN RESERVATION; PROVIDING AN APPROPRIATION;
6 AND PROVIDING AN IMMEDIATE EFFECTIVE DATE.”
7
8 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
9
10 NEW SECTION. Section 1. Allocation. Money must be allocated from the general fund to the office
11 of budget and program planning to provide reimbursement for expenditures resulting from the enforcement of
12 felony criminal jurisdiction on the Flathead Indian reservation.
13
14 NEW SECTION. SECTION 2. DISTRIBUTION OF FUNDS. THE OFFICE OF BUDGET AND PROGRAM PLANNING
15 SHALL DISTRIBUTE THE AMOUNT ALLOCATED UNDER [SECTION 1] TO LAKE COUNTY AND THE CONFEDERATED SALISH
16 AND KOOTENAI TRIBES UPON SATISFACTION OF THE FOLLOWING CONDITIONS:
17 (1) $250,000 MUST BE DISTRIBUTED ON OR BEFORE JULY 31, 2025, TO LAKE COUNTY AND $250,000
18 MUST BE DISTRIBUTED TO THE CONFEDERATED SALISH AND KOOTENAI TRIBES IF THE BOARD OF COUNTY
19 COMMISSIONERS OF LAKE COUNTY HAS RESCINDED AND VACATED "RESOLUTION NO. 22-42(A) TO WITHDRAW FROM
20 PUBLIC LAW 280" ADOPTED JANUARY 3, 2023. THE RIGHT OF THE BOARD OF COUNTY COMMISSIONERS OF LAKE
21 COUNTY TO WITHDRAW CONSENT TO ENFORCE CRIMINAL JURISDICTION PURSUANT TO 2-1-306(3) MAY NOT BE FURTHER
22 EXERCISED BY THE BOARD UNTIL THE LATER OF:
23 (A) JUNE 1, 2027; OR
24 (B) JUNE 30, 2028, IF THE TERMINATION DATE PROVIDED IN SECTION 5, CHAPTER 556, LAWS OF 2021, IS
25 EXTENDED BEYOND JUNE 30, 2027.
26 (2) THE BALANCE OF THE APPROPRIATION FOR THE FISCAL YEAR BEGINNING JULY 1, 2025, MUST BE
27 DISTRIBUTED IN EQUAL AMOUNTS TO LAKE COUNTY AND TO THE CONFEDERATED SALISH AND KOOTENAI TRIBES WITHIN
28 30 DAYS OF THE ENTRANCE INTO AN AGREEMENT BETWEEN THE STATE OF MONTANA, LAKE COUNTY, AND THE
