- 2025
69th Legislature 2025 SB0124.2
1 SENATE BILL NO. 124
2 INTRODUCED BY K. BOGNER
3
4 A BILL FOR AN ACT ENTITLED: “AN ACT REVISING ELECTRONIC LICENSE PRIVACY LAW; AND
5 AMENDING SECTION 61-5-310, MCA.”
6
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MONTANA:
8
9 Section 1. Section 61-5-310, MCA, is amended to read:
10 "61-5-310. Displaying electronic driver's license. Displaying an electronic driver's license on an
11 electronic device or handing an electronic device displaying an electronic driver's license to a peace officer
12 does not constitute consent to a search or seizure of the electronic device."
13
14 NEW SECTION. SECTION 2. DIRECTIONS TO CODE COMMISSIONER. (1) SECTION 61-5-310 IS INTENDED TO
15 BE RENUMBERED AND CODIFIED AS AN INTEGRAL PART OF TITLE 46, CHAPTER 5, PART 1.
16
17 (2) THE CODE COMMISSIONER IS INSTRUCTED TO CHANGE ALL INTERNAL REFERENCES WITHIN AND TO 61-
18 5-310 IN THE MONTANA CODE ANNOTATED, INCLUDING WITHIN SECTIONS ENACTED OR AMENDED BY THE 2025
19 LEGISLATURE, TO REFLECT THE NEW SECTION NUMBER ASSIGNED PURSUANT TO THIS SECTION.
20 - END -
